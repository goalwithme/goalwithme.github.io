GoalWithMe Support

Need help with GoalWithMe, have a question, or want to report a problem? We are here to help.

Contact GoalWithMe Support

Email: goalwithmeapp@gmail.com

GoalWithMe is owned and operated by JC Behavioral Consulting INC.

When Contacting Support

To help us understand and respond to your question, please include information such as:

  • A brief description of the issue
  • What you were trying to do when the issue occurred
  • The type of iPhone and iOS version you are using, if known
  • The version of GoalWithMe you are using, if known
  • Any relevant screenshots that do not expose unnecessary sensitive information

Please do not send passwords, Apple Account credentials, financial information, government identification numbers, or other highly sensitive information by email.

Account and Privacy Requests

For questions about your account, personal information, privacy, or account deletion, contact:

goalwithmeapp@gmail.com

As GoalWithMe develops, account-management and deletion tools may also be available directly within the app.

Safety and Abuse Reports

If you need to report concerning content, inappropriate conduct, or a suspected violation of the Safety & Acceptable Use Rules, you may contact:

goalwithmeapp@gmail.com

Please provide enough information for us to understand the concern, while avoiding unnecessary disclosure of sensitive personal information.

GoalWithMe is not an emergency service. If you believe that you or another person is in immediate danger, contact the appropriate emergency services or qualified crisis resources in your location.
